Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

PAI QuickBooks Integration ("the Application") is an internal business tool that connects to QuickBooks Online to provide financial analysis and reporting capabilities.

Data Collection

The Application accesses the following QuickBooks Online data:

  • Financial reports (Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow)
  • Accounts Receivable and Payable aging
  • Customer and vendor information
  • Invoice and payment data

Data Usage

All data accessed is used solely for:

  • Generating financial analysis and reports
  • Computing financial ratios and metrics
  • Creating visual dashboards for internal business review

Data Storage

The Application operates in read-only mode. Data is:

  • Processed locally on the user's own computer
  • Not transmitted to external servers
  • Not shared with any third parties
  • Not stored permanently (only temporary session data)

Data Security

OAuth 2.0 authentication is used for secure access. Access tokens are stored locally and encrypted. The Application cannot modify any QuickBooks data.

User Rights

Users may revoke access at any time through their QuickBooks Online account settings under Apps > Connected Apps.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the application administrator.
